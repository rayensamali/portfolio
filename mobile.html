HelpCasaMobile - Contact Management System

HelpCasaMobile is an Android application designed to help users manage their contacts with ease. The app integrates Firebase for real-time synchronization and provides an intuitive interface for adding, editing, and deleting contacts. Users can also detect and avoid duplicate entries, ensuring that the contact list remains clean and up-to-date.

Key Features

  • Real-time synchronization with Firebase
  • Contact management (add, edit, delete)
  • Duplicate contact detection
  • Cloud storage for seamless data access across devices
